Pancakes

The pancake,a timeless classic breakfast and claiming the title of breakfast in the U.S. This sweet delicicy can be enchance a million ways but lets start simple.

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

    Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. combine all the ingredients in the blender. Puree until smooth
  3. Pour batter onto the hot skillet to form 5 pancakes. Flip pancakes when edges appear to harden. Flip pancakes; cook until the bottoms are golden brown.
